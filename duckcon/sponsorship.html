DuckCon is where the people building and using DuckDB meet in person.
Sponsorship puts your company in front of them.

Silver

  • Your company name and logo on the website and on the projector screen between talks
  • Special thanks in the opening remarks
  • One piece of merchandise (one size fits all, e.g., bottle openers, baseball caps) plus stickers
  • Announcement featured in social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Bluesky) before and after the event

Gold

  • Your company name and logo on the website and on the projector screen between talks
  • Special thanks in the opening remarks
  • Three pieces of merchandise (one size fits all, e.g., bottle openers, baseball caps) plus stickers
  • Announcement featured in social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Bluesky) before and after the event
  • A marketing leaflet (up to A4) on a table in the foyer
  • Breakout room where you can connect with attendees before / after the main talks. The room is equipped with a screen or a projector for short presentations or demos
